# WorthSky / 资产星图 Support

Last updated: June 24, 2026

WorthSky, also known as 资产星图 in Simplified Chinese, is a local-first personal asset dashboard for recording assets, liabilities, exchange rates, categories, and net worth snapshots.

For support, please contact:

- Email: `worthskysupport@gmail.com`

When contacting support, please include your app version, iPhone model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue. Please avoid sending sensitive financial details unless they are absolutely necessary for troubleshooting.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does WorthSky connect to my bank?

No. WorthSky v0.1.0 does not connect to banks, brokerages, payment services, or other financial institutions. Assets and liabilities are entered manually by the user.

### Where is my data stored?

WorthSky v0.1.0 stores user-entered records locally on your iPhone. This includes assets, liabilities, manual exchange rates, reference-rate cache, custom categories, snapshots, and app settings.

### Does WorthSky upload my financial data?

No. WorthSky v0.1.0 does not upload your asset records, liability records, account names, notes, balances, portfolio totals, exchange-rate records, or snapshots to the developer.

### How does multi-currency conversion work?

You choose a base currency. WorthSky converts supported asset and liability records into that base currency when exchange rates are available.

Free users can maintain exchange rates manually. Lifetime Pro users can download generic daily reference rates and refresh them manually when needed. User-entered manual rates always take priority over automatic reference rates.

When requesting automatic reference rates, WorthSky does not send portfolio records, asset names, liability names, notes, amounts, totals, account information, or advertising identifiers.

### What is included in Free?

Free includes:

- Core asset and liability tracking.
- Up to 10 combined asset and liability records.
- Up to three record currencies.
- Manual exchange rates.
- Latest 90-day trend view.
- JSON backup export.
- Dashboard, asset, liability, and settings views.
- Themes, privacy mode, and amount display preferences.

### What is included in Lifetime Pro?

Lifetime Pro is a one-time, non-consumable purchase. It unlocks:

- Unlimited asset and liability records.
- All supported record currencies.
- Automatic reference exchange rates.
- Manual refresh and manual-rate override priority.
- Complete trend history.
- Custom categories.
- Historical snapshots.
- Monthly review.
- Currency scenario analysis.
- CSV register export.

Lifetime Pro is not a subscription.

### Can I export my data?

Yes. JSON backup export is available in Free. CSV register export is included with Lifetime Pro.

Export files may contain financial records. Please store and share exported files carefully. If you share an export file with another app, cloud service, or person, that destination's privacy practices apply.

### Can I recover deleted records?

WorthSky v0.1.0 does not include an in-app restore feature for deleted records. If you want a recoverable copy outside the app, use JSON backup export regularly and store the file safely.

### How do I restore Lifetime Pro?

Open the Pro page in the app and use Restore Purchase if Lifetime Pro is not recognized after reinstalling the app or changing devices. Restore Purchase checks purchases associated with the Apple account currently signed in to the App Store.

### Is WorthSky financial advice?

No. WorthSky is for personal asset tracking, currency conversion, and trend analysis only. It does not provide financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. All analysis is for informational reference only.

# 资产星图支持

最后更新：2026 年 6 月 24 日

资产星图（英文名 WorthSky）是一款本地优先的个人资产管理 App，用于记录资产、负债、汇率、分类和净资产快照。

如需支持，请联系：

- 邮箱：`worthskysupport@gmail.com`

联系支持时，请提供 App 版本、iPhone 型号、iOS 版本和问题简述。除非排查问题必须，请不要发送敏感财务明细。

## 常见问题

### 资产星图会连接我的银行账户吗？

不会。资产星图 v0.1.0 不连接银行、券商、支付服务或其他金融机构。资产和负债由用户手动录入。

### 我的数据保存在哪里？

资产星图 v0.1.0 将用户录入的数据保存在当前 iPhone 本机，包括资产、负债、手工汇率、参考汇率缓存、自定义分类、快照和 App 设置。

### 资产星图会上传我的财务数据吗？

不会。资产星图 v0.1.0 不会向开发者上传你的资产记录、负债记录、账户名称、备注、金额、组合总额、汇率记录或快照。

### 多币种折算如何工作？

你可以选择默认币种。只要存在可用汇率，资产星图会将支持的资产和负债折算为默认币种用于总览展示。

免费版用户可以手工维护汇率。永久 Pro 用户可以下载通用的每日参考汇率，也可以在需要时手动刷新。用户录入的手工汇率始终优先于自动参考汇率。

请求自动参考汇率时，资产星图不会发送资产记录、资产名称、负债名称、备注、金额、组合总额、账户信息或广告标识符。

### 免费版包含什么？

免费版包含：

- 基础资产和负债记录。
- 最多 10 条资产与负债记录。
- 最多 3 种记录币种。
- 手工汇率。
- 最近 90 天趋势。
- JSON 备份导出。
- 总览、资产、负债和我的页面。
- 主题、隐私模式和金额显示偏好。

### 永久 Pro 包含什么？

永久 Pro 是一次买断的非消耗型内购，不是订阅。它解锁：

- 无限资产与负债记录。
- 全部支持的记录币种。
- 自动参考汇率。
- 手动刷新和手工汇率优先。
- 完整历史趋势。
- 自定义分类。
- 历史快照。
- 月度复盘。
- 币种情景分析。
- CSV 明细导出。

### 可以导出数据吗？

可以。JSON 备份导出在免费版可用。CSV 明细导出包含在永久 Pro 中。

导出文件可能包含财务记录，请谨慎保存和分享。如果你将导出文件分享给其他 App、云服务或他人，该接收方的隐私政策将适用。

### 删除的记录可以恢复吗？

资产星图 v0.1.0 暂不提供 App 内删除恢复功能。如果你希望在 App 外保留可恢复副本，请定期使用 JSON 备份导出并妥善保存文件。

### 如何恢复永久 Pro？

如果重新安装 App 或更换设备后未识别永久 Pro，可以在 App 的 Pro 页面使用“恢复购买”。恢复购买会检查当前 App Store 登录 Apple 账户下的购买记录。

### 资产星图提供理财建议吗？

不提供。资产星图仅用于个人资产记录、汇率折算和趋势分析，不提供金融、投资、税务或法律建议。所有分析仅供信息参考。
