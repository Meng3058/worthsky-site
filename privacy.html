WorthSky / 资产星图 Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

WorthSky, also known as 资产星图 in Simplified Chinese, is designed as a local-first personal asset tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains how WorthSky v0.1.0 handles information.

Privacy contact

Email: worthskysupport@gmail.com

Summary

WorthSky v0.1.0:

  • Does not require an account.
  • Does not connect to banks, brokerages, payment services, or financial institutions.
  • Does not upload your financial records to the developer.
  • Does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking SDKs.
  • Stores user-entered asset data locally on your device.
  • Allows user-initiated exports through the iOS system share sheet.
  • May request generic exchange-rate data for Lifetime Pro without sending your portfolio records.

Information You Enter in the App

WorthSky lets you manually enter information such as:

  • Asset records.
  • Liability records.
  • Currency codes and exchange rates.
  • Custom categories.
  • Portfolio snapshots.
  • Notes you choose to enter.
  • App settings such as base currency, theme, privacy mode, and amount display preference.

This information is stored locally on your device. WorthSky v0.1.0 does not upload this information to the developer.

Account and Bank Connections

WorthSky v0.1.0 does not require a WorthSky account. It does not connect to bank accounts, brokerages, payment services, or financial institutions.

Automatic Reference Exchange Rates

Lifetime Pro can request generic daily reference exchange rates from Frankfurter, a public exchange-rate data service.

When requesting reference rates, WorthSky does not send:

  • Asset records.
  • Liability records.
  • Asset or liability names.
  • Notes.
  • Financial amounts.
  • Portfolio totals.
  • Account information.
  • Advertising identifiers.

Reference rates are cached locally on your device. User-entered manual rates always take priority over automatic reference rates.

Exports

WorthSky allows you to export data when you choose to do so:

  • JSON backup export is available in Free.
  • CSV register export is available with Lifetime Pro.

Export files may contain financial records. Exports are created only after you choose an export action and are shared through the iOS system share sheet.

If you share an export file with another app, cloud service, or person, that destination's privacy practices apply.

In-App Purchases

WorthSky offers Lifetime Pro as a one-time non-consumable in-app purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. WorthSky uses StoreKit to check purchase status and unlock Pro features.

The developer does not receive your full payment details from Apple.

Data Collection

WorthSky v0.1.0 does not collect personal data, financial data, analytics data, advertising identifiers, or tracking data.

Third-Party SDKs and Tracking

WorthSky v0.1.0 does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking SDKs.

iCloud and Sync

WorthSky v0.1.0 does not include iCloud sync or server sync. If cloud sync is added in a future release, this Privacy Policy will be updated before that feature is released.

Children's Privacy

WorthSky is not designed for children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Financial Disclaimer

WorthSky is for personal asset tracking, currency conversion, and trend analysis only. It does not provide financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. All analysis is for informational reference only.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if WorthSky adds features such as iCloud sync, analytics, account systems, support forms, additional third-party services, or changes to reference-rate behavior.

The effective date at the top of this document will be updated when material changes are made.

资产星图隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 6 月 24 日

资产星图（英文名 WorthSky）是一款本地优先的个人资产记录 App。本隐私政策说明资产星图 v0.1.0 如何处理信息。

隐私联系邮箱

邮箱：worthskysupport@gmail.com

摘要

资产星图 v0.1.0：

  • 不要求注册账户。
  • 不连接银行、券商、支付服务或金融机构。
  • 不向开发者上传你的财务记录。
  • 不包含第三方分析、广告 SDK 或跟踪 SDK。
  • 将用户录入的资产数据保存在当前设备本机。
  • 允许用户主动通过 iOS 系统分享面板导出数据。
  • 永久 Pro 可请求通用参考汇率，但不会发送你的资产组合记录。

你在 App 中录入的信息

资产星图允许你手动录入以下信息：

  • 资产记录。
  • 负债记录。
  • 币种代码和汇率。
  • 自定义分类。
  • 资产快照。
  • 你选择填写的备注。
  • 默认币种、主题、隐私模式和金额显示偏好等 App 设置。

这些信息保存在你的设备本机。资产星图 v0.1.0 不会将这些信息上传给开发者。

账户和银行连接

资产星图 v0.1.0 不要求注册资产星图账户，也不连接银行账户、券商账户、支付服务或其他金融机构。

自动参考汇率

永久 Pro 可以从 Frankfurter 这一公开汇率数据服务请求通用的每日参考汇率。

请求参考汇率时，资产星图不会发送：

  • 资产记录。
  • 负债记录。
  • 资产或负债名称。
  • 备注。
  • 财务金额。
  • 资产组合总额。
  • 账户信息。
  • 广告标识符。

参考汇率会缓存在你的设备本机。用户录入的手工汇率始终优先于自动参考汇率。

数据导出

资产星图允许你在主动选择时导出数据：

  • JSON 备份导出在免费版可用。
  • CSV 明细导出包含在永久 Pro 中。

导出文件可能包含财务记录。导出文件只会在你主动选择导出操作后生成，并通过 iOS 系统分享面板交给你处理。

如果你将导出文件分享给其他 App、云服务或他人，该接收方的隐私政策将适用。

App 内购买

资产星图提供永久 Pro 一次买断内购。购买由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理。资产星图使用 StoreKit 检查购买状态并解锁 Pro 功能。

开发者不会从 Apple 收到你的完整付款信息。

数据收集

资产星图 v0.1.0 不收集个人数据、财务数据、分析数据、广告标识符或跟踪数据。

第三方 SDK 和跟踪

资产星图 v0.1.0 不包含第三方分析 SDK、广告 SDK 或跟踪 SDK。

iCloud 和同步

资产星图 v0.1.0 不包含 iCloud 同步或服务器同步。如果未来版本加入云同步，本隐私政策会在该功能发布前更新。

儿童隐私

资产星图并非面向儿童设计，也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

财务免责声明

资产星图仅用于个人资产记录、汇率折算和趋势分析，不提供金融、投资、税务或法律建议。所有分析仅供信息参考。

本政策的变更

如果资产星图未来加入 iCloud 同步、分析、账户系统、支持表单、其他第三方服务，或改变参考汇率请求方式，本隐私政策可能会更新。

如有重大变更，本文顶部的生效日期会同步更新。
